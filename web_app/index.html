Help & Support

How to generate a recipe?

Go to the Input tab, type your ingredients and tap "Add Ingredient", then hit "Generate AI Recipe".

How to save a recipe?

Open any recipe and tap the 🔖 bookmark button in the top-right corner.

What is NutriBot?

NutriBot is your AI nutrition assistant. Tap the green chat button on the home screen to start.

Is my data private?

Yes — all data is stored locally on your device only.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

NutriSync does not collect or sell your personal data. All profile data and saved recipes are stored locally.

AI Services

When generating recipes or using NutriBot, your ingredient list is sent securely to the Gemini AI backend. No personal information is included.

Log Out?

Your data will remain on this device.
